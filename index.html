Company Profile

A Trusted Partner in the Textile Industry

SHK Weaving Technology Co., Ltd. is a leading professional weaving company founded by Taiwanese and Japanese enterprises. With over 40 years of expertise in weaving and production, we specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality woven tapes, serving both OEM and ODM business models to meet diverse customer needs.

We offer a vast range of over 500 different types of woven tapes. Our products find extensive applications across various industries, including general apparel, medical supplies, and industrial safety solutions. With our commitment to innovation and craftsmanship, we deliver woven tapes that meet the highest standards of quality and functionality.

Our rich heritage in weaving technology, coupled with our ability to adapt to evolving market demands, positions us as a trusted partner in the textile industry. We continuously strive to provide customized solutions, exceptional service, and reliable products to our valued clients.

Product Details

500+ Types of Woven Tapes & Ribbons

A complete portfolio covering elastic bands, ribbons, jacquard, shoelaces, straps, cords, belts and threads — all customizable through OEM / ODM.

Elastic Bands

Ideal for underwear, pants, baby clothes, sweaters, sportswear, wedding gowns, T-shirts, hats, bras and masks — widely used in cuffs, hems, waistbands, stockings, and sports & medical applications.

Ribbons

Suitable for climbing & camping safety belts, backpack straps, tents, personal protective equipment, fitness gear, pet leashes and collars, military / police / fire safety belts, luggage straps and more.

Jacquard Ribbons

Crafted from polyester, nylon, PP and acrylic — featuring intricate patterns, smooth texture, excellent strength, durability, colorfastness and shape retention.

Shoelaces

Fasten shoes, decorate uppers, adjust tightness and protect the ankle — providing ample space for healthy foot development, especially for growing youth.

Nylon Straps

Widely used in fashion, home textiles, footwear, gift packaging, luggage, decorations, pendant cords, pet supplies, curtains, headphone cords, eco-bags, automotive accessories, DIY crafts and more.

PP Straps

Polypropylene yarns with excellent plasticity, strong tensile strength, fatigue resistance, low density and good impact performance — versatile across many fields.

Elastic Ropes

A wide range of applications including clothing, construction, transportation equipment, bundling, sports equipment and more.

Pet Leash

Nylon construction with vibrant colors and excellent texture. Custom specifications, jacquard patterns, heat-transfer printing and softening treatments — assembled to finished leashes on request.

POLY Belt

Polymer belt materials (polyamide, polyester, polyurethane, polyether) for packaging, transportation, construction, medical and manufacturing — high strength, abrasion, corrosion and temperature resistant.

SP Thread

Twisted polyester sewing thread with superior tensile strength, luster and abrasion resistance — soft, colorfast, aging- and seawater-resistant with excellent elasticity.

Sports & Rehabilitation Waist Belts

Supportive woven waist belts for sports performance and medical rehabilitation applications.

Mask Cord

Soft, skin-friendly elastic cords engineered for medical and sanitary mask applications.
